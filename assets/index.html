About Developer Student Clubs

Developer Student Clubs (DSC) are community groups for students from any academic background in their undergraduate or graduate term. By joining a DSC, students build their professional and personal network, get access to Google developer resources, and work together to build solutions for local problems in a peer-to-peer learning environment. In short terms, it is the fastest bridge from theory to practice.

Opportunity Awaits

Join us and be one of the first students ever in North America to have access to Google's amazing Developer Student Club program! In addition to meeting and collaborating with amazing students, you can join us in a variety of other activities. We have needs for all types of talent from marketing to recruiting to developing and more!

No computer experience is necessary!

We're on a Mission

Education

Through our exclusive complimentary access to Google Qwiklabs, club members have the opportunity to earn industry recognized badges which demonstrate to the world that you've mastered a skill set with Google Cloud Platform.

Collaboration

Our dedication to incorporating the entire campus community is unprecedented in any campus club history. By utilizing the vast talents across all our collective majors we can make a truly lasting real-world impact.

Innovation

Of course, developing innovative products is a keystone of our vision. Education and Collaboration provide the foundation necessary for our technical team to bring our vision to life through the wonder of technology.

Motivation

At the end of the day our mission is simple: to bring forth positive change in our local community. The tools we've set in place will ensure that we have the opportunity to change lives and make our world a better place.
